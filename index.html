About

WILFRIED WILDE (1988) is a guitarist and composer from Saint-Sever (FR) active mainly in the jazz and rock scenes. Since the age of 18, his passion for music led him to play in various countries around Europe and more (France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Denmark, Morocco) with many different projects. As a band leader, he released the album "Oscilenscope" (2015) and co-led two albums with the bass-less trio WIZ, "Mosaico" (2023) and "WIZ" (2021), as well as with the Galician rock band LOIROS, "Back From The Edge" (2023) and "Loiros" (2019). During his ten years stay in Spain, he also had the opportunity to collaborate with many artists in studio and on stage. Additionally, he is a curated educator (10 years+), having taught music and guitar in numerous schools and conservatories. He is currently living in Basel, Switzerland.

Awards:

  • 2016: Finalist of the Getxo Jazz Festival competition leading his own quintet
  • 2011: Winner of the “Best Soloist Keep An Eye Jazz Award” in Amsterdam

Studies:

  • 2023 - 2025: Master Performance Jazz Guitar - Jazzcampus, Basel (CH)
  • 2022 - 2023: Focusyear - Jazzcampus, Basel (CH)
  • 2006 – 2010: Bachelor Jazz Guitar – Musikene, San Sebastian (SP)
