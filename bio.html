BIOGRAPHIE

WILFRIED WILDE (1988) is a guitarist and composer from Saint-Sever, France. Since the age of 18, he has played in various countries (France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Switzerland, Netherlands, Denmark, Morocco). He has shared the stage with renowned musicians such as Mark Turner, Perico Sambeat, and Javier Vercher. As a bandleader, he released the album "Oscilenscope" (2015) and co-led two albums with the WIZ projects, "WIZ" (2021) and "Mosaico" (2023), as well as with the Galician rock band LOIROS, "Loiros" (2019) and "Back From The Edge" (2023). During his ten years in Spain, he also had the opportunity to collaborate with many artists in the studio and on stage. In 2011, he won the first prize as a soloist at the Keep An Eye Jazz Award in Amsterdam and was a finalist in several prestigious competitions such as the Festival International de Getxo, the Heineken Jazz Festival, Palma Jazz Comercial, or the Concours de Jazz de Castellón. He participated in the FOCUSYEAR program at Jazzcampus Basel in the years 2022-2023, where he had the opportunity to perform with figures such as Miguel Zenón, Immanuel Wilkins, Melissa Aldana, Scott Colley, Kris Davis, Ingrid Jensen, or Wolfgang Muthspiel. He holds a bachelor's degree in jazz guitar specialization from the MUSIKENE Conservatory in the Basque Country and is currently studying for a master's degree in Performance at Jazzcampus Basel, Switzerland.
